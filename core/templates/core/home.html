Why ChoreDown?

ADHD affects roughly 1 in 10 children. These kids aren’t lazy — their brains are wired differently. They struggle with working memory (forgetting what they’re supposed to do), time blindness (not sensing how long things take), and low dopamine (needing bigger or faster rewards to feel motivated).

ChoreDown was designed around these realities. Every feature — the big colourful points badge, the one-tap task completion, the instant reward catalogue, the leaderboard — exists because structure works best when it feels like a game.

Whether your child has a formal ADHD diagnosis or just needs more visual, motivating routines, ChoreDown meets them where they are.
