Why it works for ADHD kids

Kids who struggle with routines often deal with working memory challenges (forgetting what comes next), time blindness (misjudging how long things take), and motivation that needs a visible target. ChoreDown is built around those realities.

One task shown at a time. Instant point feedback after completion. A reward system that gives kids something real to work toward. And when something goes sideways — a reset path that doesn't feel like failure.

This is not a clinical tool. It's a low-friction family system that helps reduce overwhelm, build routine habits, and make chores feel worth doing.
